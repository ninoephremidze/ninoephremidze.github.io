About

I’m a first-year PhD student in Physics at Harvard University, working with Prof. Cora Dvorkin in theoretical and computational cosmology.

Research

I’m broadly interested in using astrophysical observations to shed light on the nature of dark matter. In particular, I use the gravitational lensing of both light and gravitational waves to probe dark-matter substructure at sub-galactic scales — developing new methods, linking theoretical models to observable signatures, and probing for dark matter in real data.
